Planning for retirement can feel overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here's how to get started with confidence.

## Start with Your Goals

Before diving into numbers, think about what you want your retirement to look like. Consider:

- Where do you want to live?
- What activities do you want to pursue?
- How much travel do you plan to do?

## Understand Your Income Sources

Most retirees have multiple income sources:

1. **Government benefits** — CPP, OAS, and other government programs
2. **Employer pensions** — Defined benefit or defined contribution plans
3. **Personal savings** — RRSPs, TFSAs, and other investments
